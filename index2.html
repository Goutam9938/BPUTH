Creta EV Vehicle Details

Creta EV

The Hyundai Creta EV is an eco-friendly SUV designed to provide maximum efficiency and comfort. This electric vehicle combines modern technology, luxury, and sustainability, making it an ideal choice for environmentally conscious drivers. By operating entirely on electric power, the Creta EV significantly reduces carbon emissions compared to traditional fuel-based vehicles, contributing to cleaner air and a healthier environment. Its zero-emission design aligns with global efforts to combat climate change and promote sustainable transportation.

Specifications:

  • Battery Capacity: 64 kWh
  • Range: 450 km on a full charge
  • Charging Time: Fast charge in 1 hour, standard charge in 6 hours
  • Top Speed: 160 km/h
  • Seating Capacity: 5

Description:

The Creta EV is equipped with advanced safety features, including multiple airbags, ABS with EBD, and a host of driver-assist technologies. It features a spacious interior, cutting-edge infotainment system, and premium materials, ensuring a superior driving experience.
